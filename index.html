Dodge-A-Wrench

Keys:
  • -Use left/right arrow keys to move player
  • -Press Enter to Start/Pause Game
  • - Press 'M' to toggle music

Directions:
  • -Avoid thrown wrenches as long as you can
  • -Collect the dragonballs to get buff and survive one wrench hit without losing a life
  • -Get the highest score possible
